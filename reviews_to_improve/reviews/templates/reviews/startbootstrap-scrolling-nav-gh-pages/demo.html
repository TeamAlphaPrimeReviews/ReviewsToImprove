What we do

User reviews drive good websites. Review Improve adds to that by scanning a website's reviews, analyzing them using advanced machine learning techniques, and implementing systems that can reduce computer-generated or poorly constructed reviews. Using our state-of-the-art technology, Review Improve can provide:

  • Quality-assurance scans
  • Removal of fake computer-generated (bot) reviews
  • A dramatic increase in your sites review process
  • Enhanced user satisfaction
